This study gives an answer, ‘YES’, to the research question: whether a horizontal link is feasible. The research done so far does not aim to persuade a modeller to give up on vertical links, but to convince someone that a horizontal link is workable with more endeavours.












So far, the horizontal link is still in the stage of research, but it is significant to carry out further studies on this link. There are several potential issues to study, including correcting feedback from the 2D to the 1D, using fully 2D shallow water equations, adjusting parameters, and limiting 1D domain.
